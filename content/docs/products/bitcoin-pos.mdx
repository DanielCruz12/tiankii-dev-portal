# The Bitcoin POS - Point of Sales ⚡

### Seamless Bitcoin Transactions with Tiankii POS

Accept Bitcoin payments like a pro, on any mobile device. Start today!

---

## 🛠️ What is Tiankii POS?

**Tiankii POS** is a wallet-agnostic software designed for smartphones and POS machines, enabling merchants to seamlessly accept **Bitcoin and Lightning payments** with an enhanced user experience.

---

## 🔑 Key Features

- **Built-in NFC Contactless Payments**: Accept payments using NFC Bolt cards or QR codes.
- **Comfortable Numeric Keypad**: Simplifies input for faster payments.
- **Recent Transactions List**: View your most recent payments at a glance.
- **Tips Reception**: Accept tips directly at checkout.
- **Receipt Printing**: Print receipts for your customers easily.
- **Fiat to Sats Switching**: Display amounts in **150 fiat currencies** and switch seamlessly to Bitcoin.
- **PWA Installable**: Install Tiankii POS on any device as a Progressive Web App.
- **Peer-to-Peer Payments**: Connect your wallet for direct payments.

---

## 🔒 Enhanced Security and Control

- **Branch and Cashier Signatures**: Each transaction is signed by the branch and cashier for added control.
- **Strict Payment-Only Terminals**: Sales staff cannot transfer or spend collected funds.
- **Optional KYC**: Enable KYC (Know Your Customer) on specific terminals to gather payer information.

---

## ⚡ NFC Boltcard Integration

Tiankii POS works seamlessly with **NFC Boltcards** for **Lightning Network** payments.

- Accept fast, contactless payments with **improved efficiency**.
- Deliver a smooth and secure experience for businesses and customers.
